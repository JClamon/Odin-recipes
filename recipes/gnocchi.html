Gnocchi

Description:

This dish is easy to make, requires only a few ingredients, and is a crowd pleaser! It is best paired with the pasta sauce featured on this website.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of russet potatoes
  • 1 egg
  • salt to taste
  • flour to consistency (use King Arthur AP)

Tools Needed:

  • large pot
  • potato masher or ricer
  • potato peeler
  • strainer

Steps:

  1. rinse and boil potatoes until tender to a fork, but not mushy
  2. strain potatoes and peel while hot
  3. put potatoes through ricer or mash until smooth, no clumps
  4. crack and mix in one egg and add a generous amount of salt
  5. when homogenous, add a handful of flour and mix
  6. work it out on counter top and add flour until it reaches dough-like consistency
  7. roll into inch wide tubes and cut into inch wide squares
  8. lighty flour, then boil
  9. remove from water when gnocchi floats on top of the water for 15 seconds
  10. drain, serve, and enojoy!

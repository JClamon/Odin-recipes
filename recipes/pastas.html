Pasta Sauce

Description:

This is a super easy easy pasta sauce that is really delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 onion peeled and cut into halves
  • salt and sugar to taste

Tools Needed:

  • large pot
  • large wooden spoon
  • knife

Steps:

  1. cut onion in half and peel it
  2. place onion cut side down in the pot
  3. add in tomatoes, butter, and a large pinch of sugar in the same pot
  4. mix a little and set heat of stove to simmer, mix periodically to prevent burning
  5. cook for 45-60 minutes until fat floats free, then add salt and mix
  6. remove onion and serve!
